\# Interview - Introduction Template



* The following is a template which you should follow when introducing yourself in an Interview. You are not required to follow this template verbatim; but, it provides you with guidance on what key areas you should focus on for your introduction.



\---

My name is \[Name], a \[job role] professional with a background in \[degree/field] from \[school]. I have experience working with \[2–3 core tools/technologies, e.g. Java, C++, Python, SQL, Apache Spark, AWS, etc...], and I'm particularly interested in \[area of focus, e.g. pipeline development, APIs, database engineering, data modeling, cloud infrastructure, etc...].



Most recently, I \[briefly describe your latest role, internship, or project — what you built, what problem it solved, and the impact/scale].



(Optionally) - Before that, I \[one sentence on a previous role or notable project if applicable]



I'm now looking to \[what you're seeking — e.g. grow my skills in a production environment, work on large-scale data systems, contribute to a data-driven team], and I'm excited about this opportunity because \[one genuine reason tied to the company or role].
